About CalStacker

Free financial calculators built for India

CalStacker is a free financial education platform built for salaried employees and investors in India. We publish accurate calculators and in-depth guides so you can understand your salary, plan taxes, compare loans, and invest with confidence — without signing up or sharing personal data.

Who we are

CalStacker is operated as an independent web publisher focused on Indian personal finance. We are not a bank, stock broker, mutual fund distributor, or registered investment adviser. Our mission is to make everyday finance decisions clearer through free tools and plain-language guides.

Every calculator runs entirely in your browser. The numbers you enter are never sent to our servers.

What we publish

  • 20+ calculators — EMI, SIP, salary, income tax, HRA, EPF, PPF, FD, GST, retirement, and more
  • Financial guides — Long-form articles on salary structure, tax saving, SIP investing, home loans, and EPF
  • FAQs on every tool — How formulas work, what assumptions we use, and when to verify with a professional

Editorial standards

Our content is researched using publicly available Indian tax rules, EPFO notifications, and standard financial formulas. We update guides when major rule changes occur (e.g. new tax regime slabs, EPF interest rates). Calculator defaults reflect common Indian scenarios — your employer or bank may use different structures.

Guides are written by the CalStacker editorial team. We cite specific rupee amounts, state-level rules, and real-world examples relevant to Indian salaried workers.

Important disclaimer

CalStacker provides estimates for education and planning only. We do not provide personalised financial, tax, or legal advice. Always confirm important decisions with a qualified Chartered Accountant, tax professional, or SEBI-registered adviser. Read our full Disclaimer and Terms of Service.
