Frequently Asked Questions

Employees contribute 12% of basic + DA (subject to wage ceiling). Employers contribute 3.67% to EPF (plus 8.33% to EPS on capped wages). This calculator uses the EPF portion only.
For employees earning above ₹15,000 basic + DA, statutory PF is calculated on a maximum of ₹15,000 per month unless the employer opts for higher PF on actual salary.
EPF interest is declared annually by the government. For FY 2024-25 it was around 8.25% p.a. Rates change each year; this calculator uses 8.25% as an approximation.
Partial withdrawals are allowed for specific purposes like home purchase, medical emergency, or unemployment beyond 2 months. Full withdrawal is typically at retirement or after 2 months of unemployment.
EPF withdrawals after 5 years of continuous service are tax-exempt. Interest on employee contributions above ₹2.5 lakh per year may be taxable for high earners under recent rules.
