Exemption is the minimum of three amounts: (1) actual HRA received, (2) rent paid minus 10% of basic salary, and (3) 50% of basic (metro) or 40% of basic (non-metro). Only available under the old tax regime.
Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, and Kolkata are treated as metro cities for the 50%-of-basic limit. All other cities use the 40% limit.
