Last updated: June 2026

Overview

CalStacker.com ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and financial calculators.

All financial calculations are performed locally in your web browser. We do not require account creation, and we do not store the numbers you enter into our calculators on our servers.

Like most websites, we may use analytics and advertising services that collect standard log data such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URL. This helps us understand traffic patterns and improve the site.

We use Google Analytics 4 (measurement ID G-RR873MXQNX) to understand how visitors use our site — pages viewed, calculator usage, and general traffic patterns. This helps us improve our tools. Cookies & Advertising

Analytics and advertising cookies may be used together. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites (brokers, tax filing services, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.

Children's Privacy

CalStacker.com is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
